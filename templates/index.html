Voracious Vikings NHanes Data

We the Voracious Vikings used Google cloud, cloudycluster, python, HTML, and CSS to make graphs comparing different datasets from NHanes.

NHanes Blood Pressure Data

This bar chart shows the blood pressure levels upon different races. Throughout the years the data fluctuates. Recently in 2017 the Non-Hispanic Black Race blood pressure average has been higher than any other race.

This line chart allows us to grasp a better understanding of how the average blood pressure across the different ranges changed over the years.

NHanes Cholesterol Data

This bar chart shows how the Non-Hispanic White Race in 1999 had a higher cholesterol average by miles. Over the past few years the cholesterol levels across all ages have dialed back alot and became more even.

This line chart allows us to gather another perspective of how much the data fluctuates over the year

NHanes Glucose Data

The bar chart shows how in 1999 the averages of the different races are relatively the same. However after 2009 the averages began to fluctuate heavily. In the recent years Mexican American and Other Hispanic races have had higher Diabetes rate than the other races.

The line chart allows us to gather a better perspective of exactly how much the data fluctuates over the years.
